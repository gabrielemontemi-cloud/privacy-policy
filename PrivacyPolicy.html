1. Introduction

Beat The Tower does not directly collect personal data such as your name, email address, or home address. However, the app uses third-party services provided by Google for advertising and analytics.

The app may use the following Google services:

3. Data Collected by Third Parties

These services may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App interaction data
  • Crash and performance data

This information is collected and processed directly by Google according to its own privacy policies.

4. How the Data Is Used

The data collected by Google services may be used to:

  • Show advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Understand how users interact with the game
  • Improve app stability and performance

5. No Direct Collection by the Developer

The developer of Beat The Tower does not directly collect, store, or process personal data from users. Any data processing related to advertising or analytics is handled by Google through its integrated services.

6. Children's Privacy

Beat The Tower is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If required by applicable laws or platform rules, Google services may apply age-related protections or limitations.

7. Your Choices

You may be able to manage your advertising preferences and data settings through your device settings or your Google account settings.

8. Google Privacy Information

For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please refer to Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
