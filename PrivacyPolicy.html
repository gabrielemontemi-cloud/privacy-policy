Privacy Policy – Sonder

Last updated: March 2026

Sonder respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

Sonder does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external server. All your voice memos, locations, moods, and tags stay on your device.

2. Microphone

Sonder uses the microphone solely to record voice memos at your explicit request. Audio files are stored locally and never uploaded to external servers.

3. Location

Sonder uses GPS to anchor your voice memos to a specific place. Location data is stored locally and never transmitted outside the app.

4. Speech Recognition

Sonder transcribes your voice on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework to detect your mood and suggest tags. Transcriptions are stored locally and never sent to external servers.

5. iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled, Sonder may use your private iCloud storage to back up your data. This data is accessible only to you and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete any Echo at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

7. No Analytics, No Ads

Sonder contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics tools, and no third-party tracking of any kind.
